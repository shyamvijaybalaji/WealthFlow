Mobile App Design Brief: WealthFlow (Fintech)
App Concept: Premium AI-driven personal finance and budget planner.
Design Aesthetic: Dark Mode, Glassmorphism, 3D Data Visualization, and Modern Luxury.

Page 1: Splash & Onboarding
Prompt: A high-fidelity mobile app splash screen for 'WealthFlow'. The center features a sleek, minimalist 3D gold-line logo. The background is a sophisticated deep midnight blue gradient. Large, elegant white typography reads 'Master Your Money'. Below it, a single 'Get Started' button with a glowing emerald green gradient and glassmorphism effect. Photorealistic, 8k, iOS 18 style.
Page 2: Secure Login & Biometrics
Prompt: A secure mobile login screen for 'WealthFlow'. A prominent 'FaceID' biometric authentication icon in the center with a soft cyan glow and frosted-glass refraction. Below the icon, a clean 'Login with Password' text link. The UI features a premium glassmorphism overlay against a dark, secure-feeling background. Sharp details, high-end banking aesthetic.
Page 3: Premium Subscription (WealthFlow Pro)
Prompt: A subscription plan page featuring three tiered pricing cards (Basic, Pro, Elite) with high-end glassmorphism. The 'Pro' card is highlighted with a glowing metallic gold border and a 'Most Popular' badge. Large gold 'Start Free Trial' button at the bottom. Elegant, luxury fintech aesthetic, high-contrast details.
Page 4: Main Dashboard (Overview)
Prompt: The main dashboard UI. A large frosted-glass card at the top displays 'Total Balance: $12,450.00'. Below it, a dynamic 3D line chart shows a 30-day wealth trend in emerald green. Translucent side-by-side cards for 'Income' and 'Expenses'. A list of recent transactions at the bottom with minimalist icons. Deep navy and charcoal gradients.
Page 5: Budgeting & Category Limits
Prompt: A monthly budgeting view. The UI features horizontal progress bars for categories: 'Housing', 'Dining', and 'Travel'. Bars glow emerald green (safe), amber (warning), or red (over limit). Clean, card-based layout with soft shadows and vibrant micro-icons. Figma-inspired layout, minimalist UX.
Page 6: Savings Goals (Milestones)
Prompt: A savings goal tracker view. Features 3D holographic "bubbles" representing different goals: 'New Home' (75% full) and 'Emergency Fund' (40% full). Soft inner glows and realistic glass textures. A large floating '+' button at the bottom center. Inspiring, clean, and modern.
Page 7: Investment Portfolio & Markets
Prompt: An investment portfolio screen. A bold 3D donut chart at the top shows asset allocation (Stocks, Crypto, Gold). Below, a scrolling list of real-time stock tickers with green 'up' and red 'down' indicators. Professional, data-heavy but clean, futuristic fintech aesthetic.
Page 8: Transaction Detail & Map
Prompt: A single transaction detail page for a high-end purchase. Large currency text at the top. A minimalist map thumbnail shows the location of the transaction. Digital receipt view with glassmorphism textures and 'Split Bill' or 'Export' buttons at the bottom.
Page 9: AI Financial Insights
Prompt: A 'Smart Insights' report view. AI-generated text cards with summarized habits: 'Spending is down 12%'. Includes vibrant bar charts and a 'Predicted Future Balance' line chart. Editorial-style layout, sophisticated data visualization, high-fidelity render.
